ROSTER READY

Have you ever played on a recreational sports team?

A co-ed softball team perhaps.

It’s a lot of fun...

until you don’t have enough players.

It’s a scramble to fill an empty roster and frustrating for the team captain.

Co-ed leagues are required to fill spots with both men and women,

and it often feels like there are not enough girls and teams can be disqualified.

Coordinating a player substitute phone tree can be a nightmare and in the end,

you still can't find enough players?

Also, Rec sports leagues are expensive.

I want to play,

but I can’t commit to the whole season.

MY ROLE

I developed the login component and user dashboard using React and Semantic UI and I aided in the design of the application architecture and data models using MongoDB to support schema flexibility.

THE CHALLENGE

Create an app designed to be a hub for teams needing players and players ready to play, connecting and filling team rosters in realtime.

THE APPROACH

A landing page allows users a choice to sign in as a player or a team. A form then asks the user for all relevant info ie. contact info and location
The submit will add that listing to a realtime list where teams can browse players and players can browse teams
If the player/team meet the criteria of a user’s need they will then be prompted to sign in or sign up for an account to be able to obtain the contact information
If the player/team need is met the user will be prompted to change their status and be removed from the ready roster

THE SERVICE

Life happens and people can’t always make their team commitments, but Roster Ready makes it easy for that team to reach out to a community of participants that can help fill their need.
People that are interested in playing, deciding ‘day-of’ availability, can find teams needing players at a moment’s notice. And show up to essentially ‘save the day’.
